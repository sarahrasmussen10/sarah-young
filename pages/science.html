Nutritional Guidance

The cornerstone of our approach lies in understanding the profound impact of dietary choices on insulin sensitivity. Through prioritizing protein, minimizing refined carbohydrates, embracing natural fats, avoiding overconsumption, and implementing strategic meal timing, we'll craft a nutritional framework tailored to your needs. Together, we'll integrate these principles seamlessly into your daily routine, adjusting the specifics based on the severity of your insulin resistance.

Physical Activity

Physical activity, especially strength training, plays a pivotal role in enhancing insulin sensitivity. Whether it's structured workouts or incorporating movement into your daily life, we'll design a regimen that not only yields results but is enjoyable and sustainable in the long run.

Holistic Wellness

Beyond diet and exercise, various lifestyle factors such as sleep quality, stress management, and medication usage influence insulin resistance. By collaboratively addressing these aspects, we'll optimize your overall health and enhance your insulin sensitivity.

Lasting Change

True transformation starts with building habits. I'm committed to supporting you not only during our time together but for life. By implementing proven strategies in habit formation, we'll establish the groundwork for lasting lifestyle changes that extend far beyond our coaching sessions.

Coaching

Explore what it's like to be one of my one-on-one clients. Below, you'll find the steps to enroll in my personalized coaching program and details about what it entails.

1: Discover

Book a complimentary 15-minute discovery call to determine your risk for insulin resistance and discover if personalized coaching is the right fit for your needs. Ready to begin? Skip the discovery call and jump straight into your 45-minute initial consultation!

2: Intake Form

Once we have decided to work together, I will ask you to complete an intake form to provide insights into your health status and goals.

3: Let's Meet!

We'll schedule 12 virtual meetings, with individualized frequency. Our collaboration could span anywhere from 3 months to 1 year, with options to extend. Throughout these sessions, we'll review your progress and adapt your plan as necessary. I'll be there every step of the way, guiding you towards healthier eating, a more active lifestyle, sustainable habits, and improved insulin sensitivity.
